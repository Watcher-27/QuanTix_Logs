QuanTix Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

1. Introduction and Scope

QuanTix is an experimental artificial intelligence-powered financial analytics and educational software application developed and operated in Canada. This Privacy Policy explains in detail how we collect, use, process, store, and protect user information when you use the QuanTix application, including any associated web services, interfaces, or AI-driven financial tools.

By using QuanTix, you acknowledge that you understand that the platform is designed for educational and informational purposes only and that data processing is required for AI-based analysis and visualization of financial patterns.

2. Nature of the Service

QuanTix is not a bank, brokerage, investment advisor, trading platform, or financial institution. The application does not execute trades, manage investments, or provide regulated financial services. Instead, it functions as a software-based analytical tool that uses artificial intelligence to interpret financial behavior and publicly available market data.

The system may generate insights related to budgeting behavior, spending patterns, or stock market trends, but these outputs are not professional advice and should not be interpreted as guarantees of financial performance or predictions of future outcomes.

3. Information We Collect

QuanTix may collect the following categories of information depending on user interaction:

  • User-provided financial data: inputs such as budgets, spending categories, investment preferences, or manually entered financial records.
  • Account information: basic profile data such as username or email address if account creation is enabled.
  • Usage data: interactions with the application including feature usage, session activity, and system performance metrics.
  • Market data references: publicly available stock market data retrieved from third-party sources such as Yahoo Finance.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal identifiers such as government identification numbers, banking credentials, or passwords for external accounts.

4. How We Use Information

The information collected by QuanTix is used strictly for internal application functionality, including:

  • Generating AI-based financial insights and visualizations
  • Analyzing spending behavior and financial patterns
  • Displaying stock market trends and summaries
  • Improving system performance, stability, and user experience

We do not use user data for advertising targeting, behavioral advertising, or commercial resale.

5. Artificial Intelligence Systems and Model Usage

QuanTix uses artificial intelligence systems, including the Gemma 4 31B IT language model, to generate financial analysis and interpret user inputs. These models operate based on statistical pattern recognition and do not possess awareness, intent, or financial expertise.

AI-generated outputs may be incorrect, incomplete, or inconsistent with real-world financial conditions. These outputs are probabilistic in nature and are not validated by licensed financial professionals. Users must independently verify all AI-generated conclusions before relying on them.

6. Third-Party Financial Data (Yahoo Finance)

QuanTix integrates publicly available financial market data from third-party providers, including Yahoo Finance, to retrieve information such as stock prices, historical trends, and company-related data.

We do not control or guarantee the accuracy, timeliness, or completeness of third-party data sources. Market data may be delayed, approximate, or subject to revision. Users acknowledge that external data providers may have their own terms, limitations, and availability constraints.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

QuanTix does not sell, rent, or trade personal user data to third parties. We do not share personal financial information with advertisers or marketing networks.

Data may be processed by internal systems or AI infrastructure strictly for the purpose of generating outputs within the application. We do not knowingly disclose personal financial data unless required by law or legal process within applicable jurisdictions in Canada.

8. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards designed to protect user data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no digital system can guarantee absolute security, and users acknowledge that use of the platform is at their own risk.

Temporary data processing may occur during AI inference, but long-term storage is minimized where possible.

9. User Rights and Data Control

Users may request access, modification, or deletion of their stored data by contacting support. We will make reasonable efforts to fulfill such requests, subject to technical limitations, legal obligations, and system architecture constraints.

10. Canadian Jurisdiction and Compliance

QuanTix is operated in Canada and is intended for general informational use. The application is not registered with or regulated by any financial authority, including the Canadian Securities Administrators (CSA), and does not operate as a licensed financial service provider.

Users acknowledge that QuanTix is a software tool and not a regulated financial advisory service under Canadian securities law or related financial regulations.
