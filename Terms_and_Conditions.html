QuanTix Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

1. Overview

QuanTix is an AI-powered financial analytics application designed for educational and informational use. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect user information in accordance with applicable Canadian privacy principles, including PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act).

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information that users voluntarily provide, including financial inputs, account information, and usage activity within the application. This data is used solely to generate personalized financial insights and improve system performance.

3. How We Use Information

User information is used only for:

  • Generating AI-powered financial insights
  • Improving application performance and accuracy
  • Personalizing user experience within QuanTix
We do not use user data for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data Sharing and Selling

QuanTix does not sell, rent, or trade user data to third parties. We do not share personal financial information with advertisers or marketing networks. Data may be processed by AI systems strictly for computation and analysis purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

QuanTix may use third-party services such as Yahoo Finance to retrieve publicly available market data. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or data handling of these external providers.

6. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data from unauthorized access or misuse. However, no digital system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of stored or transmitted information.

7. User Control and Deletion

Users may request deletion of their data or account information by contacting support. We will make reasonable efforts to remove stored personal data upon request, subject to technical and legal limitations.

8. AI Processing Notice

QuanTix uses artificial intelligence systems, including the Gemma 4 31B IT model, to process and analyze user data. AI systems may temporarily process information to generate outputs, but do not “understand” or retain personal identity in a human sense.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of QuanTix constitutes acceptance of any changes.
